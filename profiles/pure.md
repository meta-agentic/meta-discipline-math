# Profile: pure

Proof-first rigor bundle. Optimize for *correctness and generality* over numbers.

- **Primary skill:** `mathematical-rigor` leads; `dimensional-analysis` is secondary.
- **Definitions before claims**, always. No worked number without a general statement first.
- **Every claim** is proved, cited (with hypotheses checked), or explicitly labelled a
  conjecture in the claims ledger. Zero hand-waving quantifiers.
- **Counterexample hunt** precedes every "always/never" claim.
- Output ends with the **claims ledger** (proved / cited / conjectured) and any open lemmas.

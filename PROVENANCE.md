All content is original and **public-safe by construction** — no instance data (repo
names, trackers, paths, promoted knowledge). The discipline draws on standard mathematical
practice; no third-party code or text is vendored.

## Selection of coverage

The *breadth* of branches covered by the nine domain skills was chosen by cross-referencing
the topic taxonomy of a public general-mathematics reference (the Wolfram|Alpha "Mathematics"
examples index) purely as a **map of which areas of mathematics to cover** — and nothing
else. No Wolfram content, notation, function names, Wolfram Language / Mathematica syntax, or
any product- or vendor-specific material is inherited, quoted, or vendored. Every skill is
original prose over standard, public-domain mathematics, and is deliberately CAS-agnostic
(`config.cas`: sympy | sage | none). Non-discipline taxonomy entries (elementary/curriculum
topics, plotting/graphics product features, "famous problems" catalogues) were intentionally
excluded as out of scope for an advanced-discipline pack.
